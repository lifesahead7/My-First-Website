About Me

Hi there! I'm a Computer Science major at Augusta University with a knack for problem-solving, a passion for racing (both on the virtual track and against assignment deadlines), and a deep love for my cultural heritage. When I'm not earning a spot on the Dean's List or juggling mathematics and cyber defense certificates, you can find me organizing events for the African Student Association, sim-racing on my Ferrari (emphasis on sim), or proudly representing the traditions of Nigeria’s Igala kingdom as the Young Attah Igala USA.

Whether it's navigating the complexities of cybersecurity or crafting the perfect sales pitch (shoutout to my time with Vector Marketing), I thrive on blending technology, creativity, and a dash of humor to tackle challenges and connect with others. And I've long believed that astronomy and humanity's desire to venture into space are the perfect tests of all the qualities we spend our lives developing—even if it's not physical. So come on! Let’s explore the cosmos—digitally of course!
